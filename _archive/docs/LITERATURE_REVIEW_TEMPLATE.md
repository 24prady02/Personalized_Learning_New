## Gaps in Literature

1. **Limited work on student-specific knowledge graphs**
   - Most work focuses on domain knowledge graphs
   - Our work addresses this gap

2. **Few studies on graph fusion for learning**
   - Most work uses single knowledge source
   - Our fusion approach is novel

3. **Limited evaluation on real student data**
   - Most evaluations are simulated
   - We plan to conduct real-world evaluation
